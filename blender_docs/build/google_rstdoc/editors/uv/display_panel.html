Display Panel You can set the editors display options in this panel. Aspect Ratio Display Aspect for this image. Does not affect rendering. Repeat Image Duplicate the image until it is repeated to fill the main view. Pixel Coordinates Display UV coordinates in pixels rather than from 0.0 to 1.0 Overlays Display As Outline Display white edges with black outline. Dash Display dashed black-white edges. Black Display black edges. White Display white edges. Faces Display faces over the image. Smooth Makes edges appear anti-aliased. Modified Show results of modifiers in the UV display. Stretching Shows how much of a difference there is between UV coordinates and 3D coordinates. Blue means low distortion, while Red means high distortion. Choose to display the distortion of Angles or the Area .
