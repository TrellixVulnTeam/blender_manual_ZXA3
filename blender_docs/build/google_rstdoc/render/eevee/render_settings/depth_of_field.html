Depth of Field Depth of field is done as a post-process effect in Eevee. The depth of field effect can be controlled in the camera settings . In the 3D Viewport, depth of field only works while in Camera View. Because of performance considerations, the viewport can exhibit color artifacts when using large bokeh sizes.
