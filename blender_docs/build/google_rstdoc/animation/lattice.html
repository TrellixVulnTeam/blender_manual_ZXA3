Lattice Lattice â€“ or commonly called deformation cage outside of Blender. A lattice consists of a three-dimensional non-renderable grid of vertices. Its main use is to apply a deformation to the object it controls with a Lattice Modifier . If the object is parented with Lattice Deform a Lattice Modifier is automatically applied.
Editing Flip (Distortion Free) Mirrors the vertices displacement from their base position. U, V, W Make Regular Resets the whole lattice to a regular grid, where the cells are scaled to one cubic unit.
