Wave Distortion Visual Effect The Wave Distortion Visual applies a wavy effects to the object.
Options Direction Sets horizontal or vertical direction for the waves. Amplitude Controls the strength and the depth of the wave. Period Controls the wave period. The time it takes to complete one cycle. Phase Shifts the wave pattern over the Object.
