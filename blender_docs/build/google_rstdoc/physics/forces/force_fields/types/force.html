Force Reference Panel Physics ‣ Force Fields Type Force The Force field is the simplest of the fields. It gives a constant force towards (positive strength) or away from (negative strength) the object’s origin. Newtonian particles are attracted to a field with negative strength, and are blown away from a field with positive strength.
