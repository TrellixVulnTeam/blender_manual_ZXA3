Bộ Điều Chỉnh Khung Dây -- Wireframe Modifier The Wireframe modifier transforms a mesh into a wireframe by iterating over its faces, collecting all edges and turning those edges into four sided polygons. Be aware of the fact that your mesh needs to have faces to be wireframed. You can define the thickness, the material and several other parameters of the generated wireframe dynamically via the given modifier options.
Tùy Chọn -- Options Độ Dày -- Thickness The depth or size of the wireframes. Dịch Chuyển -- Offset A value between (-1 to 1) to change whether the wireframes are generated inside or outside of the original mesh. Set to zero, Offset will center the wireframes around the original edges. Nhóm Điểm Đỉnh -- Vertex Group Restrict the modifier to only this vertex group. Đảo Nghịch -- Invert Inverts the vertex group weights. Hệ Số -- Factor Percentage that the vertex has influence over the final wireframe result. Miết Nếp Gấp của Cạnh -- Crease Edges This option is intended for usage with the Subdivision modifier. Enable this option to crease edges on their junctions and prevent large curved intersections. Trọng Lượng Nếp Gấp -- Crease Weight Define how much crease (0 to 1, nothing to full) the junctions should receive. Chiều Dày Đều Đặn -- Even Thickness Maintain thickness by adjusting for sharp corners. Sometimes improves quality but also increases computation time. Chiều Dày Tương Đối -- Relative Thickness Determines the edge thickness by the length of the edge. Longer edges will be thicker. Ranh Giới -- Boundary Creates wireframes on mesh island boundaries. Thay Thế Bản Gốc -- Replace Original If this option is enabled, the original mesh is replaced by the generated wireframe. If not, the wireframe is generated on top of it. Dịch Chuyển của Nguyên Liệu -- Material Offset Uses the chosen material index as the material for the wireframe; this is applied as an offset from the first material. Wireframe thickness is an approximation. While Even Thickness should yield good results in many cases, skinny faces can cause ugly spikes. In this case you can either reduce the extreme angles in the geometry or disable the Even Thickness option.
