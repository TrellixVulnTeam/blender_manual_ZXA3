Điện Tích -- Charge Tham Chiếu -- Reference Bảng -- Panel Vật Lý (Physics) ‣ Trường Lực (Force Fields) Thể Loại -- Type Điện Tích -- Charge A Charge force field is similar to a Force field except it changes it's behavior (attract/repulse) based on the effected particles charge field (negative/positive), like real particles with a charge. Which means that this field has only an effect on particles that have also a Charge field (else, they have no "charge", and hence are unaffected)!
