Trọng Lực -- Gravity Tham Chiếu -- Reference Bảng -- Panel Cảnh (Scene) ‣ Trọng Lực (Gravity) Gravity is a global setting that is applied to all physics systems in a scene. It can be found in the scene tab. This value is generally fine left at its default, -9.810 on the Z axis, which is the force of gravity in the real world. Changing this value would simulate a lower or higher force of gravity. Gravity denoted g, measurement m × s -2 . Gravity is applied in the same way to all physics systems. Gravity is practically the same around the entirety of planet Earth . For rendering scenes from the Moon , use a value six times smaller, i.e. 1.622 m × s -2 . The planet Mars has a gravity value of 3.69. The gravity value per physics system can be scaled down in the Field Weights tab.
