Color Strips This effect generates solid color frames. By default, when it is created, the color strip is 25 frames long, but you can extend it by selecting and moving one of the ends. Use this strip crossed with your main movie to provide a fade-in or fade-out.
Tùy Chọn -- Options Màu Sắc -- Color Click on the color field in the Effect panel in the Sidebar region, to pick a different color.
