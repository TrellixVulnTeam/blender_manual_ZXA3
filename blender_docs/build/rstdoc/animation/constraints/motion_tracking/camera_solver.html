Ràng Buộc Giải Nghiệm Máy Quay Phim -- Camera Solver Constraint The Camera Solver constraint gives the owner of this constraint, the location and rotation of the "solved camera motion". The "solved camera motion" is where Blender reconstructs the position of the physical, real-world camera, when it filmed the video footage, relative to the thing being tracked. This constraint only works after you have set up a minimum of eight markers and pressed Solve Camera Motion ( Movie Clip Editor ‣ Toolbar ‣ Solve ‣ Solve Camera Motion ).
