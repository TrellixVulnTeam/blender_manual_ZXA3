Nút Màn Chắn Đôi Cạnh -- Double Edge Mask Node The Double Edge Mask node creates a gradient between two masks.
Đầu Vào -- Inputs Màn Chắn Nội -- Inner Mask A mask representing the inside shape, which will be fully white. Màn Chắn Ngoại -- Outer Mask A mask representing the outside shape, which will fade from black at its edges to white at the Inner Mask .
Tính Chất -- Properties Cạnh Bên Trong -- Inner Edge Toàn Bộ -- All All shapes in the Inner Mask contribute to the gradient, even ones that do not touch the Outer Mask shape. Duy Kề Cạnh -- Adjacent Only Only shapes in the Inner Mask that overlap with the Outer Mask contribute to the gradient.
All. Adjacent Only.
Cạnh của Bộ Đệm -- Buffer Edge Giữ Nằm Trong -- Keep In Parts of the Outer Mask that touch the edge of the image are treated as if they stop at the edge. Tràn Ra -- Bleed Out Parts of the Outer Mask that touch the edge of the image are extended beyond the boundary of the image.
Keep In. Bleed Out.
Đầu Ra -- Outputs Màn Chắn -- Mask Standard mask output.
