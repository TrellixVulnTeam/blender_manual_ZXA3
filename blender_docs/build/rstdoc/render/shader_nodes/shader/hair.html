BSDF Tóc -- Hair BSDF Cycles Only The Hair BSDF node is used to add shading for Hair .
Đầu Vào -- Inputs Màu Sắc -- Color Color of the hair. Dịch Chuyển -- Offset Controls the way the light is rotated (angular shift) for the reflection/transmission. -- Roughness U/V Controls the roughness in the direction light is skewed, and perpendicular to it. Tiếp Tuyến -- Tangent Input tangent.
Tính Chất -- Properties Thành Phần -- Component There are two components that can be used to control the look of the hair. Usually you are going to want each of these and use a Mix Node . Phản Quang -- Reflection The light that bounces off the surface of the hair. Truyền Xạ -- Transmission The light that passes through the hair and comes out the other side.
Đầu Ra -- Outputs BSDF Đầu ra chuẩn của bộ tô bóng.
Ví Dụ -- Examples Nội dung cần viết thêm <2.8.
