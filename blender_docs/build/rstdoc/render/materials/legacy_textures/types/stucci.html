Stucci The Stucci texture is based on noise functions. It is often used for stone, asphalt, or oranges, normally for bump mapping to create grainy surfaces.
Options Plastic / Wall In / Wall out Plastic is the standard Stucci, while the "walls" is where Stucci gets it name. This is a typical wall structure with holes or bumps. Soft / Hard There are two methods available for working with Noise. Size Dimension of the Noise table. Turbulence Depth of the Stucci calculations.
